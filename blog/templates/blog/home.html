About

If you don't already know who I am my real name is Vihan Le Roux. I was born in South Africa and lived there for my entire childhood. I love traveling, especially long and "boring" rides across a country or state. It gives me time to think and admire the little things. On top of that, I might even see places along the journey that I did not intend to see. I like to photograph and videograph these moments and share them with the people who appreciate it. But it's not always fun. Budgeting plays a huge roll in where I get to go. My job pays for most of my trips but it is not my only source. I get sponsorships, donations and patronage from people who like my content and wish to support me. This keeps the content coming and the travels going.
